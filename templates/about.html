About VendorAI

Street Vendor Digitalization Agent powered by IBM watsonx.ai

Our Mission

India has over 10 million street vendors and micro-entrepreneurs who lack access to digital tools, formal credit, and online visibility. VendorAI bridges this gap by providing an AI-powered platform that:

  • Creates professional digital business profiles instantly
  • Guides vendors through UPI payment setup in minutes
  • Helps vendors get listed on Google Maps for free
  • Connects vendors with government loan schemes
  • Provides marketing content in local languages
Technology Stack
IBM watsonx.ai Studio IBM Granite LLM Python 3.11+ Flask LangChain FAISS Vector DB HuggingFace Embeddings Bootstrap 5 HTML / CSS / JS

How RAG Works in VendorAI

1
Upload Documents

Upload PDFs like PM SVANidhi guidelines, MSME schemes, UPI manuals.

2
Vectorize & Index

LangChain splits text, HuggingFace embeds it, FAISS stores vectors.

3
Semantic Search

User questions are matched against document vectors for relevant context.

4
Granite Generates

IBM Granite LLM combines context + query to generate accurate answers.
